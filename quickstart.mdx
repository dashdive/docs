## Set Up Your Dashdive Environment

Begin your journey with Dashdive by setting up your environment to track cloud costs effectively. 

### Initial Setup and Preview

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="cloud" title="Create Your Dashdive Account">
    Reach out to our team via. [team@dashdive.com](mailto:team@dashdive.com?subject=Create%20Dashdive%20Account). We will provision your organization in our system and walk you through the onboarding process.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="key" title="Obtain Cloud Provider Credentials">
    Dashdive needs to initialize with your cloud accounts. To start this process, you'll need to obtain cloud credentials from each provider you would like to track. This will allow Dashdive to correct for any missed events and adjust for discrepencies. Be sure to check that the cloud credentials have read access to your cloud resource.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Integrate and Deploy

<AccordionGroup>
<Accordion icon="database" title="Emit Cloud Events to Dashdive">
  Last step to start tracking your cloud costs with Dashdive is emitting cloud events to Dashdive. There are multiple ways to report events to Dashdive:

  - Automated Agent
  - Self-Service API
  - Dashdive SDK's

  For more info see [Event Ingestion](/events)
</Accordion>
<Accordion icon="dollar-sign" title="Start Tracking Costs">
  Once your cloud accounts are connected and your cloud is emitting usage events, Dashdive will begin tracking and analyzing your cloud costs. You can view and manage these insights directly from your Dashdive dashboard.
</Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
