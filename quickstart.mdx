## Set Up Your Dashdive Environment

Begin your journey with Dashdive by setting up your environment to track cloud costs effectively.

### Initial Setup and Preview

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="cloud" title="Create Your Dashdive Account">
    Start by setting up your account at [app.dashdive.com](https://app.dashdive.com). This will be your portal for managing and viewing your cloud cost data.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="key" title="Obtain Cloud Provider Credentials">
    To integrate Dashdive with your cloud accounts (AWS, Backblaze, Wasabi), you'll need to obtain your Access Key ID and Secret Access Key from each provider. This allows Dashdive to securely access and analyze your usage data.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Integrate and Deploy

<AccordionGroup>
<Accordion icon="sync" title="Connect Your Cloud Accounts">
  Link your cloud storage accounts to Dashdive through the [Dashdive Dashboard](https://app.dashdive.com/). Here, you can enter the credentials you've obtained from your cloud providers.
</Accordion>
<Accordion icon="analytics" title="Start Tracking Costs">
  Once your accounts are connected, Dashdive will begin tracking and analyzing your cloud costs. You can view and manage these insights directly from your Dashdive dashboard.
</Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
